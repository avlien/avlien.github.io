The AVLien Network

The AVLien Network (working title) is a proof of concept project to show what possibilities open source software and low-cost hosting can provide to art & music communities. It is the brainchild of Durham Bell, who drew inspiration when reading about early iterations of two related projects: Commons in a Box and BuddyPress, both social networking frameworks based on the WordPress content management system.
